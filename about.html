Our Story

Rooted in the Valley

Footehills of Alabama Realty has been part of the Tennessee Valley since 2005. We're family-owned and proudly boutique — and yes, the name is a little play on our own: the Footes, settled in the foothills of North Alabama.

Being small is a choice. It means when you call, you reach someone who knows your name and your search. It means one agent walks with you from the first showing to the closing table, not a rotating cast of strangers.

We've helped a lot of Valley families find home over the years, and we treat every one like it matters — because to us, it does.
